Who are we?

Through the Staff is the first and only organization that provides free online music lessons from conservatory-level students to young musicians across the country who could otherwise not afford or have access to them.

Our Mission

We strive to remove the inequities in access to music education to make lifelong musical journeys possible for students from all backgrounds.
