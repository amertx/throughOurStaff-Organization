Helpful
Teaching Tips

Whether this is the first lesson you have given, or the 100th, feel free to apply any of these ideas to your work.

  • Have flexible expectations
  • Remember to smile
  • Use other people’s ideas/resources/experience
  • Listen to your student’s playing and thoughts
  • Ask your students questions to get their mind thinking
  • Remain firm but positive in your student’s playing expectations
  • Create a lesson planning system
  • Plan ahead
  • Don’t be afraid to try something new or creative
  • Keep in mind: things will get easier
